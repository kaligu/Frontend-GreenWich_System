The Greenwich Library system consists of the Main Library and Byram Shubert and Cos Cob branches. With 1,800 programs and events per year, the Library serves as the cultural and intellectual crossroads of the community. Greenwich Library’s circulation is among the highest of public libraries in Connecticut and has been named a five-star library by Library Journal for 11 of the past 12 years for patron visits, circulation, use of public computers, and attendance at events.
